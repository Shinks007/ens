Entrepreneurial Strength Assessment

Be Honest with Yourself – Your Growth Depends on It

As you complete this assessment, remember that the true value of this exercise lies in your honesty with yourself. This is not a test to pass or fail but a tool to help you understand your strengths and identify areas for improvement. By selecting the most accurate rating for each skill, you gain clear insight into where you excel and where you need to grow. Only through self-awareness can you make informed decisions and take meaningful steps toward success. Be true to yourself—your entrepreneurial journey depends on it!

Rate your proficiency in each area by selecting the appropriate number (1-5).
1: Low | 2: Low-Medium | 3: Medium | 4: Medium-High | 5: High
